Who am I?

A guy who just happens love graphics design, cyber security, teaching and writing. A Muslim who strictly stands against Feminism, LGBTQ & Islamophobia.

Overview

I write, I design, I teach and sometimes I penetrate. No, not really. I'm a cyber security enthusiast and I like penetration testing. In Jun 2021, I founded IT Ishkool which is in my opinion Bangladesh’s first completely free online IT learning platform. I'm working with some of the most talented mentors out there to make sure that the students of IT Ishkool get the best guidance possible.

I also teach Linux Fundamentals. Other than that, I taught some courses regarding Cyber Security. In leisure time, I like to write. I usually write about social issues. Though I'm not involved into any kind of politics, but I have written about some political issues now and then. You can read my articles here.

Currently I'm on my way of graduation. I'm studying BSc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Bangladesh Army International University of Science and Technology (BAIUST).
